## A Multi-functional Exhibition Center

The digital comprehensive exhibition hall of Southern Investment Group, located in the Pazhou Artificial Intelligence and Digital Economy Pilot Zone. As the future "corporate reception hall" of Southern Investment Group, it aims to inspire digital intelligence and lead the Bay Area towards a low-carbon future.
